About Us

What are we doing?

We are trying to challeng the traditional stereotype of the high-tech worker. In an effort to bridge the gap between talented individuals who lack experience and the tech industry's, we provide a unique solution. Our approach involves identifying promising individuals based on their skills and potential. By assessing talent through comprehensive evaluations, we open doors for those who may have been overlooked by conventional recruitment processes.

What does the companies get?

By using our service you as a company can get access to a diverse talent pool of people who do not fit the stereotypical mold of the typical high-tech worker and by that getting new why of thinkung and neew perspectives and backgrounds can lead to more creative solutions and better products to your company

What does the employees get?

By using our service you as a juniors can get an opportunity to break into the tech industry, gain valuable experience, connect with leading tech companies, and even start a full-time job. Even if you didnt got a job the internship experience you gain through Place IL can make you more attractive to other potential employers.
